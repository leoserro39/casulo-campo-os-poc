# Case Pack Input Requirements

## Small service

Service orders, customers, schedule, payment status, pending tasks, rework examples, free text.

## Legal office

Cases, document checklist, deadlines, attendance history, fee/payment information, internal task list, free text.

## VesselFlow

Master workbook, PVQ/Q88 data, certificate/document list, contract restrictions, cargo/platform matrix, vessel sync rules, nomination rules, prior outputs/reports, known pending issues.

Minimum for first VesselFlow test: dataset/workbook, expected nomination flow, qualification rules, known blocking rules.
