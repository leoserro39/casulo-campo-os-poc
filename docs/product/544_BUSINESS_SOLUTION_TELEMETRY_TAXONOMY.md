# PROD-1701..1740 - Business Solution Telemetry Taxonomy

This phase creates a business and solution telemetry taxonomy for CASULO.

It does not perform final calibration.
It does not connect GPT.
It does not call GPT.
It does not call Codex.
It does not connect to production.
It does not authorize client-facing claims.

## Purpose

Expand telemetry beyond response boundary into business and solution context.

The goal is to understand which company profiles, solution types and service packages generate the strongest CASULO value.

## Telemetry dimensions

- company profile;
- company maturity;
- operational pain;
- service package;
- solution type;
- recommendation type;
- business delta;
- implementation risk;
- monitoring need;
- governance need;
- value hypothesis;
- stack dependency.

## Calibration boundary

This phase creates taxonomy and evidence structure only.

It is input for future calibration, not calibration itself.
