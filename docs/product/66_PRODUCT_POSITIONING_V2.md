# Product Positioning V2

## Core statement

The Cubo Operacional is an operating layer for AI programs and complex operations.

It transforms business processes, technical systems, data, evidence and software into computable state, measurable deltas, decision gates and governed actions.

## What it is not

- not a simple checklist;
- not a generic chatbot;
- not only a dashboard;
- not uncontrolled automation.

## Method

CASULO is the method. Cubo Operacional is the product surface and operating system.
