# CASULO Workbench — Product Vision

## Product name

CASULO Workbench.

## Experience name

Operational Cube Solver.

## Core thesis

The product does not start by selling automation, dashboards or AI answers.

It starts by computing operational state.

The product receives evidence, maps domains, measures fragility, creates a controlled state, shows gates, recommends smaller deltas when needed, and only releases next actions after review.

## Product sentence

CASULO Workbench is an operational state workbench that transforms evidence into a computable Cube/Cupula, measures fragility, blocks weak recommendations, and guides safe deltas toward solution.

## Product principle

Every cube move must correspond to evidence, gate, delta or state change.

## What the POC already proved

- Controlled intake.
- Evidence manifest.
- State snapshot.
- Operational graph.
- Controlled diagnostic.
- Data quality and fragility metrics.
- Human review gate.
- Controlled report.
- Runtime evidence audit.
- Internal handoff.
- Client review blocking.
- Cockpit story replay.
- Micrograph event timeline.
- Cube/Cupula UI state contract.

## What product phase must prove next

- A person can navigate the state without terminal commands.
- A case can be opened and reviewed as a product workspace.
- Cube/Cupula can explain the operational state.
- Reports can be viewed and exported.
- Human review decisions can be captured.
- The product stays safe: no client claim or implementation without gate approval.
