# Consent and Scope - Controlled Intake

Use this template before any controlled real-case diagnostic.

## Rules

- Do not include raw personal data.
- Do not include client identifiers unless explicitly authorized.
- Use anonymized or synthetic labels.
- Diagnostic is allowed only when scope says `diagnostic_allowed: true`.
- Implementation is not allowed by this intake unless a later solution gate authorizes it.
- Human review is required for confidential or sensitive evidence.

## Allowed in controlled test

- anonymized interviews
- anonymized operational sheets
- process descriptions
- generic documents without personal data
- synthetic client/customer labels

## Not allowed

- CPF, RG, personal phone, personal e-mail
- full names of clients, employees or third parties
- legal/medical/financial sensitive raw records
- credentials, tokens, passwords
